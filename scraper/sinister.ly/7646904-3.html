RE: My (Frooxius') gallery 08-01-2011, 09:43 PM #23
Thanks ^_^'

You mean the photo with light "ribbons" around? That's my younger brother on the photo :lol: I'm the one doing the lights.

Basically I set the camera shutter to a long time (20, 30, 60 seconds and such) and set the timer. Brother stays still in the position and then when the camera starts capturing I move some lights around, drawing the light path around him. I can use other camera's flash for powerful flare and illumination. Because I'm constantly moving around, I can't be seen in the photo.
