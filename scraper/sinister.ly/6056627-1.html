I personally think that it could do with some stroke, though that is just my personal opinion. Also, I think you may want to work a little bit on your text and placement of both the text and the render. They feel like they are tucked away in the corners, something which isn't that good. Try to overlap the text with the render and make it stand out alongside the render. You may also try to add C4D's and Fractals to make more effects and perhaps add some depth to your work. To do this you will also need to work a little on the light and shadows around the text and render.
