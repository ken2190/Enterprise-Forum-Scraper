Clash Royal - Deck Combinations 03-18-2016, 01:09 PM #1
Afternoon Clashers!

Couldn't find a Mobile gaming subforum anywhere so i'm assuming this is the correct place!

Interested in seeing what type of decks Sinister users use, why you use it and what are the benefits of it!

Myself i currently use, keep in mind i'm only level 6.

Giant - Level 4
Barbarians - Level 6
Knight - Level 6
Spear Goblins - Level 6
Prince - Level 2
Barbarian Hut - Level 3
Fireball - Level 4
Minions - Level 7

The objective of this deck is control your opponent, wearing them down until they have low amount of elixir/no troops on the board whilst your sitting there constantly having 3+ Elixir keeping it easy to hold your opponent down.

Theirs two combinations for this type of deck, Attack and more of a waited attack.

Attack:
Preferably wait till you have 10 Elixir at the of course, preferably getting the Giant and Prince, wait till your opponent places their troops first, then place to the opposite side they played on, (Most likely you'll take their Main Crown first has this combination is effective) Doing this takes out there first Crown without a doubt, Secondly you want to place Minions or Spear Goblins on the side they attacked you from as their troops will most likely be attacking your First Crown (Keep in mind, do this whilst your Troops are on the other side) After this you can simply place a Knight to back the Prince up, or even throw in whatever you need. The Fireballs always their if your opponent tries to counter with Skeletons/Barbarians.

Waited Attack:
This combination is based on the Barbarian Hut, same applies you want to preferably start with the Barbarian Hut, it fails Giant/Barbarians are a good Combo too buy you time. Once you get your Barbarian Hut placed, its a matter of Repetitively placing Barbarians and smaller troops like the Spear Goblins and Minions. Doing this slowly wears the opponent down as Barbarians are tough little troops.

For me this is a 7/10 Win rate, keep in mind in currently at Arena 4 (So it's a bit difficult)

I've been playing this deck style for around a week now and it's working well, My current wins are 100 and my three crown wins are at 94, meaning this Deck has a high rate for three crown wins!

This isn't a tutorial or anything i thought i'd just share a little knowledge on what type of deck i use, as I've been seeing a lot of Clash Royal activity lately on HF.

Please feel free to Drop your Stats and Decks below! would love to try out new decks and combinations,
