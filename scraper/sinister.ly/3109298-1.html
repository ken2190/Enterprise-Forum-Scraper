RE: How are staff members chosen? 08-17-2015, 12:59 AM #4
When I owned a forum, I began by opening up a recruitment thread and have members submit their applications to a private sub-forum which only allowed them to view their own application thread and no-one else's. After a year or so, I decided I would no longer allow members to submit applications, and instead, I would select a staff member based on their account traits, personality, and how they handle certain situations.
