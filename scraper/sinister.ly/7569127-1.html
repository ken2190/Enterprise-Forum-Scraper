Finished A Way Out in one sitting 03-30-2018, 05:25 AM #1
Watch the complete video play through here: https://www.twitch.tv/videos/244325499

Finished it all in one sitting. Was a fun experience but that’s definitely more down to who you’re playing with and not what you’re playing because honestly the story is silly and the gaming is minimal. However I found it way more enjoyable than the Telltale games, which feel more like an interactive DVD than a video game, and I hope we play it again with the other characters and try to get Platinum.

Yeah I’d recommend this game, but only if you approach it with good humour.

Shame it turns into Virtua Cop towards the end, mind you, with all the baddies popping up in specific locations. Didn’t mind too much though because the game was very forgiving to two people who never play FPS.

Anyone else played it?
