RE: Rate my top three signatures 10-29-2013, 10:17 AM #7
Crown is a decent looking signature from the first glance, and it shows the most balance and creativity of the 3 signatures you are currently presenting to the community. However, the lightning looks very strange because of the flare and brush effect ( the splash ) in the background. The crown takes too much attention, which makes the text un-needed to have there in the first place. The signature also lacks a very important thing and that is depth, which it lack more or less completely. The colors go partly well with each other, but the white in the crown is way to intense. The style is also a over-used, flawed and boring style to use. As it looks like its text, a shape, a flare(s) and a splatter texture/brush.

XGamer is probably the one where the lightning is the best, but then again its rather boring to look at. The lightning is flawed here too though, the ends of the flare doesn't give out any lightning like the rest of the flare does, which looks out of place and strange. This also lacks depth, even though it has some of it. Overall its not as eye catching as its all blue, and with nothing special dragging it in. The reason for this is as I've said before, the style is over-used and most people have have seen it a million times before. Therefore most people wouldn't really care enough to look at it while they are scrolling.

Star, is the most "fancy" of them all. Though it comes off as awfully cluttered and over-done. You could've done with only the star and the corner effects. The crown has nothing to do with the theme, and the Pokemon ruins the placement of the text. The reflection/shine effect is too visible and it ruins the overall quality of the signature. Again, the style is still over-used, boring and simple looking. Which drops the quality of it.
