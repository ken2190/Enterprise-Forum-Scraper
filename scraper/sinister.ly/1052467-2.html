The design isn't perfect yet but in time it will be. Everyone has their own opinions which is why it is actually neat to be able to ask for opinions on design and other stuff.

However; defending my design is what I will do. I removed most of the orange boarder this morning aside from at the top and the bottom of the page. The orange was too much and I agree with that, it never felt right to have that much of it. I substituted large portion of it with #1E1E1E which matches the darker color better and I'm still working on the absolutely correct scheme. The image posted in the thread was after the first few modifications. You might have completely different opinion when it is ready but the input is appreciated.

The old school design approach is what I am going for. It isn't supposed to be utilizing jquery drop down menus and shit like that, I just don't like it. In my opinion a theme should be comfortable and easy in usage. As I hate nothing more than themes that are complicated and resource heavy (lots of jquery and Javascript) I won't be creating anything that is flashy in design, which seems to be what you want but I don't.

This is the first time I am creating a theme for MyBB however, I've been working with web sites for several years and no stranger to the colors. I did exactly what you said I should do. I recreated another theme to understand how MyBB theme and templates system works. Now it is all up to me to create what I want. And it is getting there.
