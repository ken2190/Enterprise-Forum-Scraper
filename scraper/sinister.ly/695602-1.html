Its rather bad I have too say. Though that only means room for improvement, which is always fun to have. Anyhow, you should work on the background, you may want to use a stock image and textures for a better background as smudged ones normally become rather boring. The smudge/Motion Blur on the render is awful and shouldn't be there at all, you can use the smudge tool at the edges of the render to make it fit better if you want or you can use filters along with overlays. The lightning and depth needs work as well, though this is harder and you should look into using rendered effects and the Dodge / Burn tools.
