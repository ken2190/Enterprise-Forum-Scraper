check your profile out w/ different ranks (super easy and TEMPORARY) 05-19-2014, 12:35 AM #1
I did this as a little test for my group proposition to see how the badge looked and realised, "Hey! I could do this with other ranks!"
It's a little Chrome Element switch so it's only there until the page is refreshed.
Spoiler: here's what the test looked like

How To:
1. open element on your banner(if you don't know how, open the spoiler)
Spoiler:
2. change the image
if you clicked on the right part, you should have this highlighted

Just change it to whichever one you want and remember It's NOT permanent
Edit:
(Thanks :bluedog: for the idea)
'Google Element' is a client-side HTML editor.
What this means is, you can temporarily edit the html code of a page, changing it's code and appearance.
