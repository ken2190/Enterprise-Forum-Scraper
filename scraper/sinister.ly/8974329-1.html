Hello everyone, I've decided to make a thread showing my gallery of GFX artworks.
This was previously shown on FJ until it was taken down but now I have made the thread again and hopefully it will stay that way.
Anything I put on here has been made in Photoshop CS5.1.
Due to deletions of my previous old artworks that I made in the last few years, I will be making new ones from now on to try and improve my skills.
Please give critical feedback to any of my artworks and comment on how I could improve, not posting just a simple comment. It would be beneficial for me as a GFX Artist in the training!
I watermark my work with my symbol in the bottom right corner so people cannot steal what I have created.
Take note that I don't make RuneScape or anime signatures as they are not my area of interests. Hope you guys like them!

I will be updating this thread each time I make a new artwork so please keep that in mind!
