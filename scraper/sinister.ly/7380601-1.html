COD4 Remastered "Crew Expendable" Mission Gameplay! 07-15-2016, 05:19 PM #1


TheTechGame.com Wrote:A video giving you a look at the Crew Expendable mission from the remastered version of Call of Duty: Modern Warfare has been released.

The spiffed up version of the popular shooter comes with ten multiplayer maps from the original game, and is only included with the Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare Legacy Edition and Digital Deluxe Edition.

It will not be sold as a separate game, according to Activision.

Activision and Sony announced at E3 2016 that those who pre-order either of the aforementioned Infinite Warfare editions on PS4, would be provided with 30 days early access to the digital remaster’s campaign ahead of PC and Xbox One.

Call of Duty: Infinite Warfare will be released on PC, PS4 and Xbox One November 4.



First off, let me explain how much hype I have for this game. It was one of the first games that I kept coming back to on Xbox 360. My friends and I would play all day, go to sleep for around 4 hours, and wake back up and do it all again. I have so much memories and knowledge packed into my mind from this game, and I'm so glad it's being remastered. It was a long time coming. Needless to say, CoD4 was one of the best CoD's of all time, right next to MW2 (IMO). I don't care if I have to buy IW just to play it, I'm just glad it's being made. It looks SO DAMN GOOD. Who else has played CoD 4 back in the golden era of it's time?
