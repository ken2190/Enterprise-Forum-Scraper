Another reason was their TOS, which is overly strict regarding NSFW or unlawful content. I spent an unreasonable amount of time coordinating with their support regarding complaints over threads in the NSFW and SEing sections.

Their cash wasn't worth our time or efforts. I'd recommend using other services, such as Buysellads.
