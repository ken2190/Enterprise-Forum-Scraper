Crystal Anarchy - MyBB WIP 07-02-2014, 01:32 PM #1
Well, I've been working on a MyBB theme for about a day or so now, and I think it's time that I get some feedback on it.

I would like feedback from a few specific people seeing as I already got feedback from them before, however I have been working on the theme since then. This, however, does not mean that you can't reply if you didn't give me feedback before.

Crystal Anarchy is the name of the theme, it is a MyBB theme that is responsive in some aspects, however not all. The aim of the theme is to provide a dark, red, crystal feel, replacing most MyBB tables with it's own. A good example of this is the User CP which you will see shortly.

PREPARE YOURSELVES FOR A SHIT TON OF IMAGES.









People who I'd like some feedback from; @Customer-chan, @Schrodinger, @MiS, @Tarew, @Losimantom

Also note that anyone white bars at the top, or dotted lines along the side aren't part of the theme. The white bar at the top is my browser, and the dotted lines are my screenshot program screwing up.
