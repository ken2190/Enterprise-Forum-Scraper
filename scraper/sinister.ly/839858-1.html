[Lovely] Make A Market Place Folder On DiverseBox [Tut] 10-21-2013, 11:50 PM #1


This Tutorial is brought to you by Lovely

Well as most of you know it sucks clearing out your inbox and accidentally deleting Market deals that you may need in the future as proof for a deal dispute.

This will help you make a special folder for anything you want to make sure you save!



Go Here http://diversebox.net/private.php?action=folders

Go down to "Add New Folders"

Add a folder "Market Deals"

Press "Update Folder"

________________________________

Adding Messages To The Folder You Made


Go to http://diversebox.net/private.php?fid=1

On the left side, check mark any messages you want to move to your folder.

Change this to the folder you created. http://gyazo.com/f367a0ef2f77f3780a86221129f13820

Press "Move To"
