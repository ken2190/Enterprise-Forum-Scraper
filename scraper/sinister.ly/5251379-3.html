RE: What is your favorite video game of all time? 04-13-2016, 11:06 PM #25
Hmm, hard decision, but I'll throw down the few that I like the most.

Team Fortress 2
Got it in 2007, as I got into Steam via a hard copy of Counter-Strike Source, Day Of Defeat Source and Half-Life 2 Deathmatch that I got at a best buy, and it came with "The Black Box" I think it was called. Had Portal, Half Life 2, and TF2. Still play the game to this day, and have thousands of hours into it. Nothing else to say, the game is a good bit of fun and I love to play it with friends.

Splatterhouse
I'm assuming most people only know the newer one on 360/PS3, I'm gonna talk about the old one, but maybe I'm just being dumb. So yeah, played this one a good bit in the arcade, got good enough to clear it in one life, and I've ended up buying the board. I'm going after the rest of the cabinet (Which I keep offering a good bit of money for as it just sits in the back room of the arcade) so yeah, big fan of the game. Only issue is that because I have to run it through a Supergun and it only has SCART RGB out, I have to play it on this tiny 8" Sony PVM. Overall, the game is a good bit of fun - kinda gory, kinda spooky, got a little bit of challenge until you learn the tricks, and it's always good fun.

DOOM
I've played this game for a good while, absolutely love the community as there's more and more WADs being made for it, and the sequel is also pretty great. I have the 1st DOOM on every console except Atari Jaguar, and I love 'em all. The only DOOM I don't care too much about is DOOM 3, but I still do like it. Got a preorder on the new one, PS4 I think, so I'll probably play that a good bit when it comes out. So yeah, the games are fast (Well 1 and 2 are, not so much with 3...), they're a good bit of fun, and apart from 3 I don't need to worry about story or NPCs or anything - just good 'ol fun with a shotgun or whatever weapon I have.

Akumajo Dracula (Castlevania)
Probably wondering "Why the japanese title, eh?" and it's just because my favorite version is on the good 'ol Sharp X68000. The NES one is an absolute classic and I love it, but the Sharp X68000 with the FM Synth and MIDI-Capable sound is absolutely amazing, and the game looks even better than the NES one. Usually this game is the reason I bring out the Sharp, as even if I don't feel like playing it, I can take the MIDI-out on the Sharp, throw it into the amp, and just having the music play via the sound test is awesome. Gameplay is smooth, controls are tight, the game has a good challenge, and I just love it.

So yeah, sorry if that was a bit long-winded, I just felt I should give a little bit of explanation.

TL;DR - Team Fortress 2, 1988 Splatterhouse, DOOM, and Castlevania are probably my top four games, but I don't exactly have a favorite.
