The background for the showcase is horribly picked, the logo looks everything but fitting on it. Aside from that, then I don't really get the idea behind the logo. "Binary Options" would refer to two options, and the line fails to well.. Show that. It looks like a logo that was made without pre-thinking and just made for the sake of it. Which makes it a bad logo, as it fails in its one job. (Damnit, you had one job logo.)

Pink/Purple and Blue/Cyan isn't really a good color combination. They are too far apart on the color wheel to be a good blend and too close to be complimentary colors for contrasts. On a logo with "Binary" I'd assume contrast would be an important thing, though this logo lacks every aspect of contrast I can think of.

So I'd give it a 2/5 on the scale.
