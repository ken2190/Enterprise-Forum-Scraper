RE: Iron Man first smudge 05-19-2013, 01:16 AM #7
I think you've done a pretty good job with the background and there's a nice mix of colours. There are some areas that are a bit too bright but for the most part it's not too bad at all. Some parts of Iron Man seem a tad too sharp though, when sharpening it up try lowering the opacity of that layer so it doesn't make it look choppy. Another suggestion is to work with a slightly smaller canvas. I usually do 400x240 or so; gives you enough room to play with but not so much room that the signature ends up looking empty
