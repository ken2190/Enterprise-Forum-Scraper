RE: [Questions] Website Privacy and Security ! 08-23-2011, 01:06 PM #5
Staring a hosting company is much more than just buying some hardware. You need a decent infrastructure, fast and reliable network connections, redundancy, at least 1 decent server, not mentioning firewall and other security related hardware... you'll need a support method (phone, email) but with guaranteed support hours, you'll have to find enough customers to get ROI and, last but not least, you have to have knowledge on servers - setup, config and problem analyzing.. you'll need some monitoring as well, and so one, blabla
Like Sage suggested, start of with a vps you can rent at any decent hostingcompany - they usually rent some rackspace in a datacenter. When starting with a small budget, consider shared hosting to start with - small fees with possibility to rent some space without having to know a lot of the technical things behind a server...
