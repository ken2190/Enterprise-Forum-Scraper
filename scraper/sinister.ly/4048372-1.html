Hey guys so I am currently working on an unusual sort of URL shortener, its called Bad URL.

Basically you give it a URL and it spits out something that would make everyone suspicious and not want to click it. Its really made to play jokes on your friends. 

Here is an example of a URL

Input: google.com
Output: z8ai.cf/girlsgonewildpart1.wmv_7dx3ov_not-a-virus


So the link to the website is http://badurl.ml/ (Will get a new domain soon)

But I need the community's help, as I need ideas for blocks of text to add to the database.

Such as:
  • not a virus
  • moms movie.mp4
  • enlarge your penis
  • etc
These are the parts of text that get added in the url to make it look super dodgy. So any suggestions and I will add it into the database.
