Hacker's Helpdesk 07-17-2014, 03:03 AM #1


Hacker's Helpdesk, created by Autonomous, is a small blog dedicated to topics covering hacking tactics, security, vulnerabilities, & defensive tactics. There's a lot of coverage done by myself, & my team, on a daily basis as well as a feed pulling cyber security related information from all over the web to one central hub (all respective authors accreddited generously, of course). Additionally, because HackCommunity taught me everything I know about hacking & sparked my career-worthy interest in Cyber Security, I've set up a "Membership" area for all HackCommunity members to see every piece of information with 0 restrictions, 0 advertisements, & a fully customizable experience. Our current analytics show us that we're receiving a fairly decent following so far, mainly from social media & my fellow HC members, but I'd like to see this site grow even more.


I started this site as a project for a Cyber Security course, & decided to dedicate myself to keeping it live & a reputable spot for cyber security related information.

Since publishing the site online, we've received a lot of positive reviews mainly on the design, organization, & quality of our content. We are still looking for more authors to really get this site off the ground, so if you feel you could offer something to our site, please fill out an application! (Note: Bad grammar is an instant decline. You're applying for a writing position ..don't use "u" "2", & other half-assed abbreviations).


Hacker's Helpdesk's blog will consist of video collectives, reviews on tools & distros we've previously used, general notes & anything else we find intersting on our ventures!

Our "Ask A Hacker" section is a collaborated idea for a section socially available for other hackers to be able to answer any questions in an organized "forum" type environment. I'm in the process of implementing this feature,& its looking great, always I'm definitely looking for suggestions on how to improve it!

Of course, the tutorials section is self-explanatory, but what I'd really like to do is allow other authors to post as well somehow. Again, just an idea still in progress.

We are currently looking for authors & other positions for the site, if you're interested you can visit the "Apply" section, or click the link in my signature!


I'd like to thank anyone still reading here, & I hope you'll take a moment to check out my site! I'll be keeping this thread up to date as the site grows, if you review the site, I'll be sure to post your testemonial here as well!
