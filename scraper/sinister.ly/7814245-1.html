RE: Supreme Wallpaper Showcase 07-05-2016, 01:20 PM #5
I am not a fan of either to be honest. I'll give you a few reasons why though, just so this message isn't so barren and so that you can actually get some constructive feedback. (Totally not talking you guys that have replied earlier.) Unto the parts I do not like; 

The wings in the background, while they are fairly good pen-tool wise, they aren't really fitting with the name. "Supreme" refers to a being or object that is of the highest rank. If you had angel wings in your mind while making them, then cheers for the idea. Though the end result looks nothing like angels wings, they look like demon wings or imp wings. (Yes I know, we are all small devils in here, but still.) Aside from the vibe they're giving out then another complaint would be the random cut at the end in the middle. They don't "end" they just "stop" right away. No transition into an end or anything. Then you have the bad try at depth with the second set of wings, fading the ones behind isn't really depth. 

(It's a poor try at best.) Using shadows is depth, as you already have the light source. (Actually fading tends to look bad on wallpapers and logos.) This 'depth" also crashes with the reflection of the text, which is also badly done. There is no reflection of the wings, which makes no sense as there has to be a surface right under the main text, that he wings just clip through. (The highlight line at least makes it appear that it is.)  The logo text itself could need some depth work, so it doesn't look so flat. Add in some shadows and highlights to it, maybe some pattern over and across it?

I like the whole idea and how well the wings are made with the pen tool, but Inton has already listed most of what I'll say so I'll just go ahead and say my constructive criticism.
