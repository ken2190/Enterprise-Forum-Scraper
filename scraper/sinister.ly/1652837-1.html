Did anyone enjoy playing Fable as much as I did.
Fable:
  • I think the first one was the best because when you became evil it changed your appearance more than the other two games.
  • The magic in this one was the best out of the three. It was so cool to upgrade and was very powerful.
Fable II:
  • The second one had more weapons and more areas but I always felt drawn back to the first one.
  • The amount and how the magic worked in this one disappointed me.
Fable III:
  • Then the third one was like hundreds of years after the first one and the character couldn't even use magic anymore, he had to wear gloves to use it.

Overall I liked them but the first one was my favorite.
Anyone else have any opinions about them?
