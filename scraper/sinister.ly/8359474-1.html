Call of Duty 2016 Confirmed in Development by Infinity Ward 02-16-2016, 12:55 AM #1

Call of Duty 2016 has been confirmed to be in development by Infinity Ward, the developers of Call of Duty Ghosts that was released in 2013 during the launch of PS4 and Xbox One. We still don’t know whether their new game is COD: Ghosts 2, COD: Advanced Warfare 2 or a sequel to an older Call of Duty like Modern Warfare. Call of Duty Ghosts was the weakest entry in the series. It was developed by Infinity Ward and released in 2013 for the PS3, PS4, Xbox 360, Xbox One, PC and Nintendo Wii U. It was riddled with bugs at launch and also faced harsh criticism over its multiplayer and single player content.

The next Call of Duty was from Sledgehammer Games who released a brand new entry in the Call of Duty series called Advanced Warfare. It was released in 2014 for the PS3, PS4, Xbox One, Xbox 360 and PC. The game was an improvement over Ghosts in almost every way.



[ MY THOUGHTS ]
Spoiler:
In my opinion, CoD has gone downhill since BO2. I think a lot of us agree on this. However, I feel like I shouldn't give up. IMO, I think that's what makes a game successful. When I played MW2 for the first time, I played that shit non stop. I got CoD Ghosts and sold it within a week. That's pretty poor.
