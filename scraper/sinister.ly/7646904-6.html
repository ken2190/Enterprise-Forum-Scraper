I have enough money to actually buy the Photoshop (even the full CS version), I just choose not to, because I don't need it and it would be a waste of money. I don't do warez, first I myself, having developed some software, amateur films and other stuff respect work of others and if it's really useful to me, I pay for it, because it has some value to me and secondly, I also do some commercial stuff here and then, so it both is better to get the tools I use legally and it also pays itself off quickly.
