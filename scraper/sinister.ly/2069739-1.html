MyBB Support

I've been using MyBB since the year the platform was released. I've helped hundreds of clients build their MyBB forums from the roots on up. Me and Omniscient from HF also go way back and we both worked together on several of the plugins that he's officially released on his site. MyBB is natural to me - and I'm a proud Guru of the system.

If there are any questions you have regarding plugins, installation, security, or anything else MyBB related leave them in the comments section and I'll try to quote you as soon as I can. I'll try keeping this thread updated with some popular FAQ's as well.

If you feel uncomfortable getting help from me you can always refer to the official MyBB site.
